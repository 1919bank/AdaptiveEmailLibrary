2

Customize Your Design

Modify colors, layout, and content using our intuitive MJML syntax. No deep HTML knowledge required. 


    <mjml>
      <mj-body>
        <mj-section>
          <mj-column>
            <mj-text>
              Hello World!
            </mj-text>
          </mj-column>
        </mj-section>
      </mj-body>
    </mjml>
3

Build & Test

Use our build tools to compile your MJML into production-ready HTML. Test across different email clients with our integrated testing tools. 

npm run build
4

Deploy & Send

Your email is ready to go! Use our export options to send via your favorite email service provider or download the HTML directly.
